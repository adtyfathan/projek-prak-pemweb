Enjoy a Visa-Free Trip to China!
Good news! China grants visa-free access to citizens of 38 countries, including Australia, France, Germany, Hungary, Ireland, Italy, Spain, Switzerland, Austria, Malaysia, Singapore, and more . If you're a citizen of any of these countries, you can visit China visa-free for up to 30 days, until the end of 2025.
